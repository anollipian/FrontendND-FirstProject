Healthy Hot Chocolate

October 20 2021

Servings: 1 People

Total Time: 5 min

Ingredients List:

  • 2 TBS Cocoa Powder
  • 2 Tbs Honey
  • 1 Cup Milk

Directions:

  1. Mix Honey & Cocoa Powder Together till they form a sauce.
  2. Heat the milk till it becomes warm.
  3. Pour the warm milk over the chocolate sauce.
  4. Mix Together & Enjoy with a slice of cake.
