Spinach & Strawberry Salad

Main Page

October 15 2021

Servings: 6 People

Total Time: 20 min

Ingredients List:

  • 1/2 lb Spinach
  • 1lb Strawberries
  • 1 teaspoon dried parsley leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground oregano
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Directions:

  1. Wash & cut Spinach, Strawberries & Cucumbers.
  2. Combine the herbs in a shallow bowl with salt and pepper and oil.
  3. Mix the Veggies with the seasoning.
  4. serve with toast.
